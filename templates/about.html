What is Gutenkraft?

Gutenkraft is a non-profit technology research and development (R&D) outfit focused on tackling infrastructure related problems in developing nations. The name is a portmanteau word for 'good strength or good force'. The components of most national infrastructure today are bulky, difficult to deploy and maintain, making it hard for developing nations to provide necessary national facilities. We are working to create and implement a technology driven blue-print that the developing world could use to address her infrastructure woes and to reduce perpetual reliance on foreign aid. The aim is to drive national growth and advancement for sustainable improvement of living standards, by innovating on and adapting technologies to develop and deploy the necessary building blocks of key growth infrastructures. This is codified in our mission and vision.

Our Mission:

To make national infrastructure technology solutions sustainable and affordable for developing nations.

Our Vision:

To produce affordable and sustainable world class infrastructure technology solutions on a large scale.

Gutenkraft Ops

We have seven operational focus areas (Ops) that we consider essential to our objectives and projects. Each op consists of a grouping of talents/skills specfic to it and which serve as in-house sounding-board that players in each project consult or enlist in. Expertise from various ops unit, would occassionally work in concert towards implementing solutions for specific problem of focus in a Gutenkraft project.
