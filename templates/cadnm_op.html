Computer aided design and manufacturing (CAD&M) Ops

The focus of this op is to muster and/or develop expertize in Open source Electronic design automation (EDA) and 2D/3D CAD softwares for fabless semiconductor development and design of precision engineered systems respectively. An initial focus will be on EDA tools such as,

  • KiCAD
  • LibrePCB

For CAD&M initial tool of focus include,

  • FreeCAD

The recent breakthroughs with machine learning (ML) especially in Generative Artifical Intelligence (AI), creates a whole new level of possibilities in terms of competence augmentation. In the CAD/M space, Generative Design, which can be used to create and evaluate a wide range of design options based on design requirements(constraints) and objectives, can reveal innovative concepts and reduce time and cost of design. At Gutenkraft, the CAD&M Op is also focused on developing compentence in the generative design space to leverage AI systems in driving innovation and eficiency in the solution design.

The above focus would make it handy for Gutenkraft to quickly develope system prototypes and provide workable blueprints/schematics, IP cores/netlists and etching masks for mechanical and semicondutor hardware production respectively.
