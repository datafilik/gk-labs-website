Computer aided design and manufacturing (CAD&M) Ops

Our Electronic Design & CAD Operations focuses on cultivating expertise in open-source Electronic Design Automation (EDA) and Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software, enabling both fabless semiconductor development and precision-engineered systems design.

In the EDA domain, we prioritize proficiency in industry-standard tools including KiCAD and LibrePCB. For Computer-Aided Design and Manufacturing (CAD/M), we have established FreeCAD as our primary development platform.

Recent advancements in Machine Learning (ML), particularly in Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI), have unlocked unprecedented opportunities for enhancing design capabilities. In CAD/M applications, Generative Design enables the rapid creation and evaluation of diverse design solutions based on specified requirements and constraints, facilitating innovative concepts while reducing design cycles and associated costs. Our CAD/M operations maintain a strategic focus on developing expertise in generative design methodologies to leverage AI systems for enhanced innovation and design efficiency.

This comprehensive approach enables Gutenkraft to rapidly develop system prototypes and deliver production-ready assets, including:

  • Detailed blueprints and schematics
  • IP cores and netlists
  • Precision etching masks for semiconductor fabrication
  • Mechanical hardware production specifications
