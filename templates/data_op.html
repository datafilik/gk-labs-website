Data Ops

The usual troupe about data is along the line of data being the 'new oil'. This is stated as a testament to it's value in revealing actionable insight for both good and bad purposes. But such insight reveal opportunities for introducing efficiencies to the systems from which there were collected.

The data ops unit is focused on data warehousing and analytics for creating models to ensure better understanding as well as to seek out opportunities to make systems much efficient.
